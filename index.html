Our Story

  1. July 22nd, 2015

    Molly and Isaac went on their first date. They met up for a drink at Onieals

  2. September 24th, 2015

    After a night out in Little Italy for the San Gennaro festival, the two officially became a couple; the kids call this "DTR" (Defining the relationship).

  3. 2015 - 2017

    Birthday parties, date nights, concerts, holidays, homecomings and goodbye drinks with friends and family.

  4. March 15th, 2017

    Spending almost every moment together, Molly and Isaac decided to save some money and move in together. They found a cute little apartment in the Lower East Side with a balcony so they could watch dumplings being prepared across the street at their soon-to-be favorite dumpling spot, Shu Jiao Fu Zhou

  5. November 2nd, 2018

    After grabbing some dinner at a recently opened Thai restaurant, Noree Thai Bazaar, The duo walked to nearby Onieals for a drink. On the way, huddled under their shared umbrella, they reminisced on the great times they've had together. Starting from July 22nd, 2015, they named the milestones in order until Isaac got down on one knee and said:

    ...And on November 2nd, 2018, we got engaged

  6. We're excited to share many more milestones together
